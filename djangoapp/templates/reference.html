Healthcare Value Resources

Granular Cost Data Websites

Website Description
Healthcare Blue Book Provides a "Fair Price" for any of a variety of medical procedures, tests, and office visits, based on nationwide data and customized by zip code. Also has some quality data.
Guroo Provides prices and quality measures for a variety of tests, office visits, and procedures as well as care bundles.
Fair Health - Consumer Website/ Consumer website arm of a larger organization dedicated to health care value and decision-making for consumers. Includes estimated charge, reimbursement, and cost data
California Office of Statewide Planning and Development All hospitals in California are required by law to have published chargemasters (i.e. the charge list they use). Other states have passed similar laws.
R-Scan Radiology specific data.
Castlight
Amino

 

National Organizations on Cost and Value

Organization Description
Costs of Care An NGO dedicated to better care at lower cost.
Choosing Wisely Website with Top 10 lists of low-value care to avoid
Healthcare Cost Institute (HCCI) Some great reports and data on cost of care
Lown Institute An advocacy group dedicated to reducing the overall cost burden. Has a great weekly email letter called Right Care Weekly.
High-Value Practice Academic Alliance (HVPAA) A new organization looking to have an academic take on high-value care. Has a pediatric arm.
Academic Pediatric Association - Special Interest Group on Healthcare Value A group interested in this topic in a pediatric specific way. Great way to get to know other pediatricians with this interest.

 

Databases on Cost and Value

Database Description
Healthcare Cost and Utilization Project (HCUP) A part of AHRQ, provides a variety of different databases on quality and cost related measures. Does not contain actual cost and charge data (I am pretty sure)
AHRQ's Data Resource Page A link to several resources including patient surveys on costs of medical care
Center for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS) Healthcare Expenditures Datasets on national health expenditure for the U.S.
Center for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS) Cost Reports Datasets on national healthcare costs for the U.S.
Organization for Economic Cooperation and Development (OECD) International comparisons of healthcare costs as well as outcomes data
World Bank Data Source A great resource for all kinds of data. Type in "Health" in the search box and see what comes up.
NIMH Mental Healthcare Cost Data
PHIS - Pediatric Health Information Systems Comparitive pediatric billing database
